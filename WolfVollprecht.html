Wolf is currently pursuing his master in Robotics, Systems and Controls at ETH Zurich. But he's always had a knack for Open Source. while working on JET, a framework to compile NumPy, he was really missing a library like xtensor. And when Wolf saw the powers that wizards of template metaprogramming hold, he wanted to learn it too. What better way than to contribute to xtensor!
