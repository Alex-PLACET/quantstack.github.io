Joël Lamotte
Scientific Software Developer

@Klaim

Joël is a Scientific Software Engineer at Quantstack, sperialized in C++, embedded software and related domains.

Before joining QuantStack, Joël worked since 2003 in various companies covering a large spectre of domains all dominated by performance constraints: games, embedded software of all sorts, robotics (Softbank-Robotics/Aldebaran), "heavy" interractive web apps (https://jellynote.com score player for example)...

Through the years Joël participated to various open-source projects with, in recent years, a particular focus on dependency management for C++ projects (like build2) and the WebAssembly platform.

As one of the organizers of the CPPP conference and CPPFrug (C++ French User Group) meetup he also participate to the C++ community and discussions about it's evolution.

Joël is also a game developer, music composer and comics maker.
