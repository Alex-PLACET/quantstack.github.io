Hongguang Zhu
Scientific Software Developer

Hongguang is a Scientific Software Engineer at Quantstack. He obtained a Master degree in image processing from University of Rennes 1 in 2010 then he went to Canada(QC) to work on research project about autonomous vehicle. During that time, he became interested in scientific computing then worked on a few research projects in France related to open-source scientific computing such as the parallelization of linbox (a linear algebra toolbox for cryptography) and the improvement of scatnet(a machine learning package for seismology).

Hongguang discovered the critical role of open-source work in science and is keen to contribute back to a community that gives so much.
