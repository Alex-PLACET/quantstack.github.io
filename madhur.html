Madhur Tandon
Scientific Software Developer

@mad_tandon | @madhur-tandon

Madhur is a Scientific Software Developer with QuantStack. Besides core data science and machine learning, he is interested in tools that enable and enhance a data scientist's workflow and experience. He also has a knack for Podcasts, Entrepreneurship, Design and Open Science.

Prior to joining QuantStack, he has worked with Mozilla, Deepnote, INCF (International Neuroinformatics Coordinating Facility), TCS Research and Elucidata.

His current work focuses on expanding the scientific C++ ecosystem in JupyterLab by developing new visualization libraries such as XVega and also contributing towards democratizing anaconda through Quetz.
