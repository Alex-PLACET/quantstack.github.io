Careers at QuantStack

QuantStack is seeking talents in the open-source scientific computing community. Join a team committed to open-science and free software.

We are passionate about science and technology and strive for a positive impact. We are the people behind major open-source tools used to advance science from classrooms to research labs and industry.

We are an equal-opportunity employer and we encourage applications from everyone, including members of all equity-seeking communities.

Robotics Full Time Internship or Working Student

June 4th 2021

QuantStack is one of the leading contributors to scientific open source software in Europe. We work on scientific visualization software in 2-D and 3-D, interactive computing with Python and C++, and tools for robotics development.

We are looking for a motivated robotics software development intern or working student to join us to work at the interface between robotics, perception, machine learning, visualization and data science. We are currently focusing our robotics efforts to help launch robots in the cloud where we work closely with the ETH spinoff Rapyuta Robotics.

The internship may begin at any time during the year. Interns are expected to join the team for a minimum of six months. The preferred location is Berlin (Paris is an option), working hours between 20 hours (working student) or 40 hours (full time internship).

Responsibilities.

  • Work on challenging problems at the interface of robotics and data science
  • Be self-motivated and contribute to our open source stacks
  • Integrate yourself into our open source community by helping out others
  • Challenge the status quo and come up with new solutions
Qualifications.
  • Basic programming knowledge: C++, ROS, JavaScript or Python are a plus
  • Background in mechanical engineering, robotics, electrical engineering, computer science, or related field with a strong academic record
  • Good English skills
  • Structured, detail-oriented, and proactive work style
  • Excellent team and communication skills
Benefits.
  • Work in an interdisciplinary, international team of highly skilled people
  • Have a significant impact in a small, agile company
  • Global visibility in the open-source and scientific software community
  • Opportunity to travel and present at conferences such as EuroSciPy, ROS & PyData conferences and meetups

Where we can hire

Our engineering team is fully remote. The company is headquartered in Paris, France, and the majority of our team is Europe-based. We will consider applications in the European Union.

Send a short CV and cover letter to careers@quantstack.net
